# Transferring Ownership of a Report

[Previous](t_reports_build_revise.htm) [Next](t_reports_running.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

Required system privileges: Transfer Ownership of Reports and Add/Edit/View/Delete Reports.

1.  In Reporting and Analytics, click the side navigation menu, click Reports, click Builder, and then click the report you want to transfer.
2.  Click Owner.
3.  Select a new owner and then click Save and Close or Save.
