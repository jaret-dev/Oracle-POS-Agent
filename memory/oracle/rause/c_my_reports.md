# My Reports

[Previous](t_categorygroups_hierarchy.htm) [Next](t_manage_my_reports.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

Use this space to easily find your preferred reports. The system administrator can add up to 150 reports that are most relevant for your role. You can add up to 150 more reports to this page.

The My Reports page gives you the option to view only your favorite reports by clicking the favorites icon ![This image shows the Favorite button.](img/favorites.png "This image shows the Favorite button.").
