# Define System-Level Privileges

[Previous](t_roles_add_role.htm) [Next](t_subj_areas_assign.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

Define and place limits on what each role can do within Reporting and Analytics.

1.  In Reporting and Analytics, click the side navigation menu, click Administration and then click Roles.
2.  Click the role you want to update.
3.  Click Privileges and select system privilege options.
4.  Click Save and Close or Save.
