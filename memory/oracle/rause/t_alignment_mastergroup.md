# Configuring a Master Group

[Previous](t_alignmentrules_creating.htm) [Next](t_alignment_masterstore_setting.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

1.  In Reporting and Analytics, click the side navigation menu, click Configuration, click Warehouse Admin, click Miscellaneous, and then click Master Groups.
2.  Click Add, or select a master group and then click Edit.
3.  Enter a name, suffix, and comment for the master group, and then click Save.
    
    Reporting and Analytics appends the suffix to items aligned with the master store. This lets you immediately identify the alignment status of items when performing manual alignment. For example, if you configure the Front of House master group with the FOH suffix, and then set a master item for COCKTAIL, Reporting and Analytics appends the suffix and shows the item as COCKTAIL.FOH.
