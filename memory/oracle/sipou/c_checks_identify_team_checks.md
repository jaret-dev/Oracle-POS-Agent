## Team Check Identification

Team checks are denoted by the letter T at the workstation. The Pick Up Check SLU shows the letter T in the tab of the block representing the team check.

Figure 3-1 Teams Checks on the Pick Up Check SLU

  
![This figure shows the appearance of team checks in the Pick Up Check SLU.](img/teamcheck_slu.png "This figure shows the appearance of team checks in the Pick Up Check SLU.")  

The Pick Up Check by List dialog shows the letter T in the Table column.
