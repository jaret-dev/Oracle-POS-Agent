## Combo Meals and Groups

A combo meal is comprised of two or more menu items grouped together and sold to customers for a special price. Quick service restaurants sell combo meals to increase business by packaging the most frequently requested menu items (for example, hamburger, fries, and a drink) and offering them for a lower price than à la carte items.

Simphony creates a combo meal using two or more combo meal groups. In the following figure, for example, you must add a menu item from each combo meal group to create a pizza combo meal.
