# Removing a Service Team From an Open Team Check

[Previous](t_checks_add_service_team_to_check.htm) [Next](c_checks_identify_team_checks.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

## Removing a Service Team From an Open Team Check

You need to have the appropriate privileges to perform this task. To remove a service team from a check:

1.  Pick up the appropriate team check.
2.  Press Remove Team From Open Check, and then press OK to confirm.
