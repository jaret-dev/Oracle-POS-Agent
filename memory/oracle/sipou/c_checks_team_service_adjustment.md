## Team Service and Check Adjustment

You cannot add, remove, or edit a team on a reopened closed check or when adjusting a closed check. When an adjusted or reopened closed check is finalized, Employee Tip Report totals adjust accordingly to reflect the changes in the check. For example, if a charged tip was modified in an adjust closed check of a team check, when the adjustment is complete, Simphony updates the charge tip totals on the Employee Tip Reports of the team members.
