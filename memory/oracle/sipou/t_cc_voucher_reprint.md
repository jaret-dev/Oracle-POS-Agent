## Reprinting a Credit Card Voucher

You can reprint the credit card voucher for a closed transaction in the current or previous business dates, and you can reprint a credit card voucher for the last transaction on the current workstation. You need to have the appropriate privileges to perform these operations.

1.  To reprint the credit card voucher for a closed transaction:
    1.  Reopen the closed transaction.
    2.  Press the Reprint CC Voucher function key.
2.  To reprint the credit card voucher for the last transaction on the current workstation:
    1.  Press the Reprint Previous Credit Card Voucher function key.
    2.  (Optional) If there are multiple credit card charges present on the last transaction, select the desired credit card.
    3.  A single copy of the credit card voucher prints.
