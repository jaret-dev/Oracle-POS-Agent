# Cashing Out Gift Cards

[Previous](t_svc_redeem_card.htm) [Next](t_svc_issue_multiple_cards.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

## Cashing Out Gift Cards

1.  Navigate to the gift and loyalty screen, and then select the function key to cash out a gift card.
2.  Swipe the card or enter the card number.
3.  Pay the card balance as cash due to the guest.
