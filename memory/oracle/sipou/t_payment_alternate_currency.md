# Tendering with an Alternate Currency

[Previous](t_payment_multiple_credit_card.htm) [Next](c_paying_menu_item_opera_self_pay.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

## Tendering with an Alternate Currency

1.  Pick up a check, and then press the touchscreen button configured for the foreign currency. For example, if the customer is paying in Euros, press the Euro function key.
2.  Take note of the amount due in Euros, and then press OK.
3.  Press the Cash function key, enter the amount that the customer paid in Euros, and then press OK.
