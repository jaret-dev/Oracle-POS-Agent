# Emailing Customer Receipts to Guests

[Previous](t_checks_multiple_language_printing.htm) [Next](c_check_filter_and_memo_check.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

## Emailing Customer Receipts to Guests

1.  Sign on to the workstation
2.  Start an order and add menu items.
3.  Tap the appropriate button to process payment for the order.
4.  On the receipt prompt, tap Email Receipt.
5.  Ask the guest to enter their email address on the CFD and then tap Send Receipt.
6.  If the customer leaves without entering an email address, tap Cancel Email Receipt.
