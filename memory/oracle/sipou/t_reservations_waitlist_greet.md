## Greeting the Guest

The Greet Guest feature allows hosting staff to greet guests who arrive earlier than anticipated or with reservations on future days. You must have the Greet Wait List Entry privilege to use the Greet Guest feature.

1.  Double-tap quickly on either the Reservation List or Wait List to open it.
2.  Long-press on the request, and then select Greet Guest. If a reservation request is not present on the Wait List, it moves from the Reservation List to the Wait List. When the hosting staff selects Greet Guest, a future reservation appears on the Wait List, but the reservation record does not move to today’s date.
