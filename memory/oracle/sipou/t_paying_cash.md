# Tendering with Cash

[Previous](c_paying_check.htm) [Next](t_payment_cash_back.htm) JavaScript must be enabled to correctly display this content

## Tendering with Cash

1.  Click the Cash function key.
2.  When tendering exact cash, click OK on the Change Due dialog.
3.  When tendering partial cash, enter the Cash Amount on the Cash dialog, and then click OK.
